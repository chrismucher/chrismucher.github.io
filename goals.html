If re-elected to the Board of Trustees, I will make the following projects my priorities for the next four years:

  • Adoption of a township Long Range Comprehensive Land Use Plan
  • Work with Zoning Commission to revise township zoning code to reflect new Comprehensive Plan’s long range goals
  • Focusing efforts to promote economic development within areas of Miami Township designated in the CEDA agreement signed with the Village of Yellow Springs
  • Continue work with Community Resources to establish new Commerce Park to bring jobs and energy to Miami Township and Yellow Springs
  • Continued work with the Tecumseh Land Trust to acquire conservation easements for farmland preservation within Miami Township
  • Continue efforts to strengthen township budgetary process
  • Work with Miami Township Fire Department to acquire a new water tanker truck in 2012
  • Continue work on feasibility study for the building of a new firehouse
  • Better efficiency for maintenance at Clifton-Union cemetery
  • Continue efforts to further enable public use of the historic Grinnell Mill
  • Research companies that can provide more cost effective insurance coverage
  • Increase participation in non-emergency cooperation for public works projects with neighboring townships
  • Continue active participation with the Greene County Township Association
