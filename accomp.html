In an open letter (viewable here) I detail my case for re-election.

Here is an additional list of some of my accomplishments while serving the township over the past 15 years:

  • Current president and four-term past president, Miami Township Board of Trustees
  • Proposed and oversaw establishment of Township Budget Reserve
  • Proposed and co-authored Cooperative Economic Development Agreement with the Village of Yellow Springs
  • Initiated regular joint meetings with Yellow Springs Village Council
  • Proposed and oversaw establishment of Farmland Preservation Fund to partner with the Tecumseh Land Trust to obtain over 2200 acres of conservation easements in Miami Township
  • Three-term past president, Greene County Township Association
  • Two-term past vice-president, Greene County Township Association
  • Two-term past secretary, Greene County Township Association
  • Current seven-term representative to Greene County Regional Planning and Coordinating Commission
  • Current two-term representative to Miami Valley Regional Planning and Coordinating Commission
  • Member Greene County Farmland Preservation Committee
  • Current six-term representative Greene County Health District Advisory Committee
  • Current representative to Miami Township/Yellow Springs Community Resources Committee
  • Current representative to Miami Township/Yellow Springs Economic Sustainability Commission
  • Charter member Yellow Springs Sourcewater Protection Committee
  • Whitehall Farm Preservation Taskforce member
  • Proposed and wrote Miami Township Personal Policy Manual
  • Approved over $467,000 in resurfacing of township roads
  • Oversaw hiring of new Township Zoning Inspector
  • Recruited three new members to Township Zoning Commission and Board of Zoning Appeals
  • Recruited new Township member to Yellow Springs Village Planning Commission
  • Approved purchase of two new EMS Department Ambulances, new Fire Truck and new Rescue vehicle
  • Proposed and oversaw update of Township computer system
  • Proposed and oversaw adoption of Township Zoning Code up-date for PUD and Cell-tower Districts
  • Proposed and oversaw adoption of up-dated Township Zoning Map
  • Transcribed Township Zoning Codes onto computer files
  • Proposed and wrote new Township Nuisance Property Ordinance
  • Proposed establishment of Township Long Range Comprehensive Land Use Plan
  • Proposed and negotiated acquisition of historic Grinnell Mill from Antioch College
  • Served as Chairman of Grinnell Mill Restoration Committee
  • Current Township representative to Grinnell Mill Foundation
  • Established recycling program at Yellow Springs firehouse
  • Initiated expansion and organization of Township records archive facilities
  • Proposed and oversaw painting and new security locks at Yellow Springs and Clifton firehouses
  • Proposed and oversaw new building signs and security lighting at Yellow Springs firehouse
  • Proposed and oversaw new heating system for Clifton firehouse
  • Proposed and oversaw repainting Township Road Department building
  • Proposed, designed and wrote all text for new Miami Township website, www.miamitownship.net
  • Currently update all Township meeting minutes onto Township website
