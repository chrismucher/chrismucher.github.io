Chris was born in upstate New York, second in a family of four children. At the age of nine his family moved to northern Ohio where he lived in the town of Hudson until attending Ohio University, in Athens, where he received his Bachelors degree in 1976. In 1980 he moved to Miami Township with his wife Cindy Struewing where they owned and operated the Tastee Freeze in Yellow Springs until 1986. From 1985 to 2006 Chris operated a second business, Movieworks/Photoworks, providing on-site film processing and video movie rentals. Chris and Cindy have three wonderful children, Christen, Stephen and Mike.

Chris was appointed Miami Township Trustee in November 1996. He successfully ran for election in 1997 to complete the unexpired term and again ran successfully in 1999, 2003 and 2007 for a full four year term as trustee.
