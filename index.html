Welcome Friends

It has been my pleasure to serve as a Miami Township Trustee these past 19 years, and I'm proud of what we've been able to accomplish together. This includes securing conservation easements for 2200 acres of Township farmland, saving the historic Grinnell Mill, and opening the new Glen Forest Cemetery and Natural Burial Grounds expansion.

In addition to supporting ongoing efforts such as conservation, budgetary, and other efficiency projects, it has always been my goal to provide the best facilities and equipment for our excellent Miami Township Fire/Rescue and Road Departments. If re-elected, I'll continue to support the Township's Comprehensive Land Use Plan, and work with the Village of Yellow Springs to promote economic development in Miami Township as outlined in the Comprehensive Plan.

With your help, I would like to continue putting my knowledge and experience to work for Miami Township. Thank you for your ongoing support, and for your vote on November 3rd.

Sincerely,

About Chris

Chris was born in upstate New York, the second child in a family of four. At the age of nine, his family moved to Hudson, in northeast Ohio. He lived there until attending Ohio University, where he graduated with a Bachelor's degree in History and Government. In 1980, he moved to Miami Township with his wife Cindy, who grew up in Yellow Springs. There, they raised three wonderful children: Christen, Stephen, and Mike.

From 1980 to 1986, Chris owned and operated the Tastee Freeze. From 1985 to 2006, he ran Movieworks/Photoworks, which provided video rentals and on-site film processing. When his kids were young, Chris coached the Reds, Marlins, and Mariners in Yellow Springs Youth Baseball. From 1993-2001, he was Cub Master and Assistant Scout Master for Yellow Springs Boy Scout Troop #78.

Chris was appointed Miami Township Trustee in November 1996, and he was elected to complete the unexpired term in 1997. Chris has been re-elected to four full terms since 1999. He currently serves as President of the Board of Trustees of Miami Township, and is an active member of the Ohio Township Association, the Ohio Cemetery Association, and the Society for the Preservation of Old Mills.
