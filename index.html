Welcome

Dear Friend,

Welcome to chrismucher.com, a resource I've put together to help inform voters about myself and my views for Miami Township. It has been my pleasure to serve as township trustee for these past 19 years, and with your help I'll be able to continue working on behalf of our township. If re-elected, I'll focus my efforts to provide the best facilities and equipment for our township Fire/Rescue and Road Departments. Additionally, I will continue to support the adoption of a Comprehensive Land Use Plan and continue the partnership with the Village to promote economic development within areas of Miami Township designated in the CEDA agreement.

Take a few moments and browse my website. The accomplishments section below highlights the projects that I've undertaken and completed while serving as your township trustee.

Thank you for your vote and your support on November 3th.

Sincerely,
Chris Mucher

About Chris

Chris was born in upstate New York, second in a family of four children. At the age of nine, his family moved to Hudson in Northeast Ohio. He lived there until attending Ohio University, where he received his Bachelors degree.

In 1980, he moved to Miami Township with his wife Cindy where they owned and operated the Tastee Freeze in Yellow Springs until 1986. From 1985 to 2006, Chris operated a second business, Movieworks/Photoworks, providing on-site film processing and video movie rentals. Chris and Cindy have three wonderful children: Christen, Stephen, and Mike.

Chris was appointed Miami Township Trustee in November 1996. He successfully ran for election in 1997 to complete the unexpired term and again ran successfully in 1999, 2003, 2007, and 2011 for four year terms as Miami Township Trustee. He currently serves as President of the Board of Trustees.
